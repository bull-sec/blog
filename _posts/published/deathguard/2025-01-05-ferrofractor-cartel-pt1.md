## The Justification

At some point ~~last year~~ in 2024 I bought a Myphitic Blight-hauler on a whim.

![Myphitic Blight Hauler](https://www.warhammer.com/app/resources/catalog/product/920x950/99120102080_ETBDeathGuardBlighthauler01.jpg?fm=webp&w=920&h=948)
*Aren't they the cutest things?!* 


I don't even remember buying it, or who/where I bought it from, it showed up at my workplace in an unmarked brown package and I proceeded to leave it in my desk drawer and then completely forget about it.

A number of weeks later I was having a clear out of the desk drawer in an effort to look busy because I knew one of my colleagues was looking for someone to do some very important work, that I had absolutely no interest in getting involved in whatsoever. The parcel came home with me that evening, and I ended up building the push-fit mini almost as soon as I got home. The lil' fella sat on my desk watching over me, and I now suspect, subtly infecting me with the desire to spend money on miniatures...

To cut a long and tedious story short, I spent the next couple of months picking up minis, and eventually ending up with a whole Death Guard army festering on my shelf.

Well it's now 2026, 2025 was a bit of a write-off, I barely got anything done due to mental health issues. But I'm back now and I'm much more motivated, so it's time to tackle this fairly mammoth project once and for all.

> I did manage to get some stuff done, I've assembled 95% of the vehicles, walkers, and minis as well as done most of the kitbashing and basing. The Plague Marines, most of the infantry ,and a few of the Characters have been primed and had a bit of work done on their base coats. I had started painting the Poxwalkers as well before I crashed out... those are semi-done in a box behind me.

## The Army List

The full army list is now as follows:

| Number | Type                    |
| ------ | ----------------------- |
| 1      | Typhus                  |
| 1      | Biologus Putrifier      |
| 1      | Foul Blightspawn        |
| 1      | Icon Bearer             |

| 1      | Noxious Blightbringer   |

| 30     | Poxwalkers              |
| 21     | Plague Marines          |
| 3      | Deathshroud Terminators |
| 1      | Foetid Bloat Drone      |
| 3      | Myphtic Blight Hauler   |
| 2      | Chaos Spawn             |
| 1      | Land Raider             |
| 1      | Predator Annihilator    |
| 2      | Rhino                   |
| 1      | Plague Burst Crawler    |
| 2      | Warning Dog Karnivore   |
| 1      | War Dog Brigand         |

All of the above comes to just shy of 3000 (2920) pts, which is what I was aiming for. I didn't want to build to 2000 pts and then have the list be broken by some random points change or model removal, so by building to 3000 I've ended up with loads of choices and a very (*I think*) Death Guard-y feeling warband.

Two caveats to the above list. The first one is that I don't (as of the time of writing) own the Deathshroud Terminators yet, so they'll be purchased at some point down the line. The second caveat regards the "Land Raider"... which, while it is technically derived from a Land Raider STC, it's actually a [Spartan Assault Tank](https://www.warhammer.com/en-GB/shop/legiones-astartes-spartan-assault-tank-2022).
