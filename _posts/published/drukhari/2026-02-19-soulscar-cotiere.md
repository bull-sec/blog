Up until recently, one of the best value boxes GW sold was the Drukhari Combat Patrol. It was one of the very few boxes where multiple purchases made a pretty decent amount of sense. GW recently changed it up, and now it's only really worth picking it up if you're into the Haemonculus Coven side of things. The old one was quite the collection of minis for the price (damn you inflation and the ongoing war in Ukraine), and because it was basically core infantry and some transports it was great starting point for a new player, and someone looking to build an army. RIP old Combat Patrol.

### Old Combat Patrol Contents

- 1x Archon
- 10x Kabalite Warriors
- 5x Incubi
- 1x Raider
- 1x Ravager

The Ravager/Radier is basically the same kit, and it's apparently rather simple to magnetize the weapons so you can essentially have both options available depending on what you want to do in a given game. And due to the unique way in which the Raider/Ravager kits are built, with all the Kabalites hanging off the side, you can essentially kitbash yourself another 10x Kabalites should you choose to.

Literally the only downside to purchasing more than one box was getting multiple of a fairly average looking Archon mini, and then having multiple Archons to paint. Which as problems go... is not that bad...

TL;DR old Drukhari Combat Patrol = Stonks

To cut a potentially long story short, I was in Element Games one day with money burning a hole in my pocket when I spotted two on the shelf, and quickly added them to my little plastic basket. Those went almost straight into storage, but over the past 18 months or so I've been slowly building up some more boxes for the army, and I now have over 2k points of spiky little sadists. I've also been collecting the Combat Patrol Magazine by Hachette, and that had a whole gang of the Aeldari jet bikes, which look like they're easily converted into Drukhari Reavers (of which I have 9 already!), which would make it a VERY jet bike heavy warband, and I kinda like that idea.

The only mini I have left that I want to pick up for this army is a Voidraven Bomber. For one it's such an awesome looking thing, and it also looks like it would be a great little painting project with potential for some creativity, but that's something for down the line once I clear off the backlog a bit. So currently the army will be built in some sort of order that I've not quite worked out yet, from the following list of boxes:

- 2x Combat Patrol boxes
- 1x box of 10x Kabalites (bought this on a whim for Kill Team v2 but never did anything with it)
- 1x box of 10x Wyches
- 3x boxes of Reaver Jet Bikes
- 2x Talos/Cronos
- 2x Venom
- 1x Killteam: Hand of the Archon (basically fancy Kabalites)
