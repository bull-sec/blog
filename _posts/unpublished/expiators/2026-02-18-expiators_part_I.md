What do you do if you enjoy the general theme of the Black Templars Chapter, but shy away from GW's official Chapters because it limits creativity? Oh and also you're a bit of a Chaos enjoyer at heart.

You find a way to make a really convuluted middle ground, and call the results of these mental gymnastics "The Expiators".

## What is an Expiator?

Think Warren G and the song "Regulate"... just kidding. (RIP Nate Dogg)

Expiate is derived from the Latin expiāre, which meant "to atone for" or "to appease". It's all about atoning for sins, but the twist with "The Expiators" is that they consider themselves to be pure beings, free of sin, and it is **you** that needs to atone for your sins. Or more specifically, it is the Imperium and it's citizens.

> "*The Galaxy is a filthy place, and it is our duty to cleanse it*" - Brother Chaplain Marrius

## Where did the Expiators come from?

They are actually Black Templars, but they are a splinter faction that believes that the Chapter is weak, and filled with fakes and noisy hypocrites.

## OK, but why should I care about them?

Because they are my latest homebrew creation, something I've been plotting in the back of my mind for quite a while now, and they will be the focus of what I assume will be a fair amount of posts on this site.
