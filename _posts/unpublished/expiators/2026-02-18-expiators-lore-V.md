The plot is completely lost by all members of the Chapter in response to the deaths of their Brothers and they commit a series of escalating atrocities. The violence is only halted when the bloodshed attracts the attentions of the Warp and a Daemon appears to partake in the slaughter. It's sudden appearance causes the attentions of the Crusaders to be diverted, and after the ensuing battle the battered remnants of the Crusade stop and hear nothing but silence. They consider this a sign that they have done their duty, and head back to the ship in orbit.
