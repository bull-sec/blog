A random citizen decides that enough is enough and straps a load of explosives to their chest and blows themselves up as an Expiators procession passes through a tight network of streets on the planet of Andonis. This has the opposite of the intended effect, and the Crusade takes that as their cue to enact horrible retribution against the remaining populace that had, mostly, accepted their new regime and it's Crusaders with gusto (it was either that or be killed, so ya know, easy decision).

The story ends with the start of what would become the massacre (the majority of which will happen "off-screen" because fuck writing about all that kind of stuff, better to alude to things and let peoples imaginations fill in the blanks)
